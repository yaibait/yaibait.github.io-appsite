Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

Welcome to Game Booster Power. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Device Performance Data: To optimize your gaming experience, our app monitors local device metrics such as CPU usage, RAM availability, battery temperature, and network latency (Ping). This data is processed strictly locally on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Installed Applications: We access the list of installed applications on your device solely to identify games and allow you to launch them through our dashboard or apply the VPN filter.

2. Local VPN Usage

Game Booster Power utilizes Android's `VpnService` to create a local, device-only VPN profile. The sole purpose of this VPN is to block background network access for non-gaming apps while you are playing, ensuring your game gets maximum bandwidth. We do not route your internet traffic through external servers, nor do we monitor, collect, or log your network activity.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as Google AdMob for advertising and Google Play Billing for in-app purchases). These third parties may collect device identifiers and usage data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Permissions

  • Do Not Disturb: Used to block intrusive notifications while gaming.
  • Draw Over Other Apps: Used to display the real-time performance overlay.
  • VPN Configuration: Used exclusively for local bandwidth management.

5. Data Security

Because we do not transmit your performance or gaming data to external servers, the risk of data breaches from our end is virtually zero. Your data remains on your physical device.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
