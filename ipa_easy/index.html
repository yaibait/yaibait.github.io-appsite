Description

IPA Easy helps you master English pronunciation step by step with the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA). Through interactive charts, real-time feedback, and gamified practice, you can improve both your listening and speaking skills.

With IPA Easy, you can:

Explore the complete IPA chart with clear audio samples in both British and American English.

Record your voice and get instant AI-powered feedback on your pronunciation.

Practice building words and sentences from sounds.

Play quizzes and mini games to test your skills.

Track your learning progress and focus on difficult sounds.

Perfect for learners who want to speak English clearly, confidently, and naturally.

Key Features

✅ Interactive IPA chart (vowels, consonants, diphthongs) ✅ British & American audio samples ✅ Voice recording + AI feedback ✅ Word-building & sentence practice ✅ Quizzes and pronunciation games ✅ Progress tracking & reminders ✅ Simple, lightweight design

Why IPA Easy?

Unlike other apps that only show IPA symbols, IPA Easy connects symbols → sounds → words → sentences, giving you a complete path to better English pronunciation.
